## Key learnings

1. **Holdout methodology exposes optimization-time leakage.** Current champion ranked #457 of 1152 on truly held-out data. Without the holdout, this leakage is invisible.

2. **Parameter tuning alone cannot defend against regime turns.** Both champions lost in 2025 (-13% to -16%). The 2025 turn is structural to the breakout mechanism on NSE.

3. **A 30-line config change (regime exit) > 1152 parameter sweeps.** The regime exit alone delivered the +35pp 2025 swing that two months of optimization couldn't.

4. **Mechanism diversification gives free Sharpe.** Correlation 0.532 between eod_breakout and low_pe. Ensemble Sharpe (1.538) > either solo. Textbook diversification working.

5. **eod_breakout's edge is concentrated in 5 of 17 years.** Top 20 instruments = 81.5% of profit. Adani group alone = 20.5%. Forward-return is highly path-dependent on whether the next concentration shock comes again.

6. **Path-dependence matters.** Same params on 2010-start vs 2018-start gave different 2025 results (-13.20% vs -1.18%) because of accumulated portfolio state at the boundary.

7. **CAGR ranking misleads.** Current top-5 sorted by CAGR includes regime-fragile strategies. By Sharpe (forward-realistic), `eod_breakout` (1.334) and `low_pe` (1.198 with Cal 1.016) are the most deployable.
